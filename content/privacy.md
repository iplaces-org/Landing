## Introduction

At iPlaces, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

## Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways, including:

### Personal Data
When you register for an account, we may ask for your name, email address, and location preferences to personalize your experience.

### Usage Data
We automatically collect certain information when you visit our platform, including your browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you have viewed.

### Location Data
With your permission, we may collect and process information about your actual location to provide location-based recommendations and features.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain our platform
- Personalize your experience and deliver content relevant to your interests
- Improve our website and services
- Communicate with you about updates, features, and promotions
- Monitor and analyze usage patterns and trends

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Export your data in a portable format

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@iplaces.org.
